Michael Young
Introduction of Designer

ARTDNA brand pays attentions to the development of era and the changes of consumption demands, insists on integrating fashion aesthetic into products design, and advocates the concept of low-carbon designs. The low-carbon design concept is aimed at prolonging the service life of the products to minimize the impacts on the environment. ARTDNA brand officially signed a contract with Mr. Michael Young, an well known industrial designer, to help the design and quality of ARTDNA products reach a new height.
